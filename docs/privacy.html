Capitillian

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

Capitillian does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device and does not require an account or sign-in.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not track your behaviour across other apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

Capitillian stores game progress locally on your device — including your badge counts, daily play history, and streak. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Capitillian offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock unlimited daily plays. Purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Crash Reporting

The app uses Sentry for anonymous crash reporting. Crash reports may include device type, iOS version, and a stack trace. No personally identifiable information is included. This data is used solely to fix bugs.

Third-Party Services

Capitillian uses the following third-party services:

— Sentry (crash reporting): sentry.io/privacy

— RevenueCat (purchase management): revenuecat.com/privacy

— Apple App Store (distribution and payments): apple.com/legal/privacy

Children

Capitillian does not knowingly collect data from children or anyone. The app is appropriate for all ages.
