Geography Quiz App for iOS

Learn Every Country in the World

Capitillian is a geography quiz app for iPhone that teaches you world capitals, country flags, populations, and map locations through a fast-paced card matching game played on an interactive spinning globe.

Whether you're studying for a geography exam, training your memory, or just embarrassed that you can't find Hungary on a map — Capitillian makes learning geography genuinely fun.

  • Match countries to their capitals — all 197 countries
  • Learn world flags and national populations
  • Explore regions: Africa, Asia, Europe, the Americas, Oceania, Caribbean
  • Interactive globe map that zooms to every country you select
  • Hardcore mode — one wrong answer ends the game
  • Earn badges for perfect rounds. Legendary tier: 100 flawless games
Privacy Policy

What We Collect

Almost nothing.

  • Game progress — stored locally on your device only. We never see it.
  • Crash reports — sent to Sentry if the app crashes. Includes device type, OS version, and a stack trace. No personal information.
  • Purchase receipts — processed by RevenueCat and Apple. We receive only whether your entitlement is active. We never see your payment details.

We do not use analytics. We do not track sessions. We do not sell data. There are no accounts.
